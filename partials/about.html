about us

PTALA is the in-house marketing arm of a large real estate investment firm and specializes in the marketing of brands within our iconic venues nationwide, including Washington DC’s nationally renowned Union Station, Faneuil Hall in the heart of downtown Boston, Beverly Connection retail center in Los Angeles, Harborplace lifestyle center and mall in Baltimore’s inner harbor, San Antonio’s unique Rivercenter mall on the famous Riverwalk, and The Bravern, in Seattle's highly sought after Bellevue neighborhood where the advertising opportunities are second to none.

With a current reach in excess of one hundred million people per year, PTALA offers one of a kind experiences to any advertiser looking to truly dominate in any of our markets, whether it be through creating an out-of-home execution, complete takeover of one of our properties, one-of-a-kind experiential event, pop-up store, in-window execution on premises, interactive displays, stunts or prop display, sponsorship/naming rights campaigns, and even the production of large-scale events such as Earth Month 2013 in Union Station.
